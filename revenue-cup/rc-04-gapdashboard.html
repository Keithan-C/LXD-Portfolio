Revenue Cup · Design thinking
Gap analysis & community dashboard
Why ideas stay invisible — and how we designed to change that. Plus a live community dashboard for tips, hidden Power BI features, and feedback.
Skill gap
Primary
Teams can't build a data-backed business case. Power BI sits open but unused — not because they don't want to, but because nobody showed them which three views actually matter.
Motivation gap
Primary
No channel existed from idea to incubation. If trying leads nowhere, people stop trying. The gap isn't apathy — it's the absence of a reason to act.
Habit gap
Secondary
Opening Power BI isn't a habit for non-technical teams. One exposure isn't enough. The Revenue Cup creates four weeks of repeated, low-stakes practice with real stakes at the end.
Environment gap
Root cause
No structured pathway from insight to incubation existed. Fix the environment first — motivation, habit, and skill follow naturally.
