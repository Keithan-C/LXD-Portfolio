How I work

Six principles, applied to every project

01
Performance consulting first
Gap analysis before design. Environment, motivation, and skill gaps treated separately. Training is rarely the first answer.
02
Interactive prototyping
Working HTML artefacts — not mockups. Stakeholders see and feel the experience before a single session runs.
03
Simulation design
Decision-based scenarios with designed failure states. Learning happens when people make real choices with real consequences in a safe environment.
04
Behaviour change metrics
Baseline before day one. Delta at day 30. The design is accountable for the outcome — not for completion rates.
05
Scalability built in
Facilitator guides, async modules, train-the-trainer — designed from the start, not bolted on when the client asks for it.
06
AI-assisted design
Claude and other AI tools used throughout — for rapid prototyping, scenario generation, and interactive artefact development.
