The Revenue Cup

Performance-centred innovation challenge · Keithan Cokbilir

Ideas don't stay invisible because people lack creativity. They stay invisible because there's no structured pathway from idea to incubation — and no reason to believe that trying leads anywhere.

The Revenue Cup is not a training programme. It's an environment redesign. Four weeks, a live scoreboard, peer voting, and a real incubation prize — designed to surface revenue ideas from teams who already have them but no channel to share them.

If they don't use it — I've failed.

The Revenue Cup is not designed to produce participation rates. It is designed to produce data-backed ideas from teams who already have them — and a pathway for the best one to become a business case. If no ideas enter incubation, the environment design failed, not the people.
