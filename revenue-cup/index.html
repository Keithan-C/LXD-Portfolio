How the pilot works — 4 weeks
Week 1
Anchor to live work
Each team names a real supplier relationship, active project, or live process their idea connects to. No data required yet.
Week 2
Scoreboard goes live
Ideas appear on a live scoreboard. Peer voting opens. Effort becomes visible across the function.
Week 3
Data strengthens ideas
A one-page job aid releases — 3 views that turn operational data into a commercial argument. Released when motivation is highest.
Design decision
Week 4
Pitch day
Top 3 ideas presented to leadership. Winner enters 6-week structured incubation with a named owner.
